MAZE RUNNER

Story

In a dimly lit maze, the player, armed with only a map and courage, navigates winding paths and hidden traps. Coins are scattered throughout the maze, glinting faintly in the shadows, tempting the brave adventurer to collect them. Along the way, keys are needed to unlock hidden paths, each key bringing them closer to freedom. But danger lurks – bots patrol the corridors, moving with mechanical precision, ready to chase if the player gets too close. With each key collected, the player’s hope grows, the exit shimmering like a distant star. Finally, the player spots the gate, a diamond-encrusted portal that promises escape. But one last key remains, hidden in the depths, the final challenge before victory.
